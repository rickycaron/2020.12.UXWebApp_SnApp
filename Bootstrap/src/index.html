Who's cooking tonight ?

A potluck is a gathering of people where each person or group of people contributes a dish of food prepared by the person or the group, to be shared among the larger gathered group. (Wikipedia)

This Potluck site is made after an example given in the demonstration videos for the UXWD course. This page is made by groep 6 after getting to know the basics of HTML and CSS. the example page can be found on the server studev.groept.be
