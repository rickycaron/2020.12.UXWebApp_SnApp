Who's cooking tonight ?

A potluck is a gathering of people where each person or group of people contributes a dish of food prepared by the person or the group, to be shared among the larger gathered group. (Wikipedia)

This Potluck site is specially designed as demonstration for the UXWD course. In the end, this page can be found on the development web server studev.groept.be Within this demonstration we will step-by-step create this site.
